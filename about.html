Our Story

Shoes Made By People Who Actually Wear Them

Nexora is a footwear studio obsessed with one thing: making shoes that feel as good as they look. From the road to the street to the trail, every silhouette we design starts with a real problem we wanted to solve for ourselves.

From One Workshop to Six Shoe Families

Nexora began in 2019 in a small workshop with a single prototype and a simple frustration: most "performance" shoes weren't comfortable, and most "comfortable" shoes weren't durable. We set out to fix that.

Today our lineup spans six distinct shoe families — from the Air Core Pro running silhouette to the rugged Velora Trail Edge — but every pair still goes through the same obsessive testing process our very first prototype did.

We design in-house, source materials we'd be proud to stand behind, and ship every order the way we'd want to receive it ourselves.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Craft Over Shortcuts

Built to Last
